🍩 Churros Recipe

Delicious Spanish Dessert

What are churros?

Churros are a sweet fried dessert from Spain. They are crispy on the outside and soft on the inside. They are usually covered with sugar and sometimes cinnamon.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 1 cup flour

Instructions

  1. Boil water, sugar, salt, and oil
  2. Add flour and mix until dough forms
  3. Let the dough cool
  4. Put dough into a piping bag
  5. Fry in hot oil until golden brown
  6. Roll in sugar

History of Churros

Churros are a traditional dessert from Spain. Many people believe they were invented by Spanish shepherds who lived in the mountains. They needed simple food, so they made dough from flour and water and fried it.

Another theory says churros were inspired by a Chinese dish called "youtiao". Portuguese sailors brought this idea to Europe.

About Spain

Spain is a country in Europe known for its rich culture, delicious food, and warm climate. The capital city is Madrid.

Spanish cuisine includes famous dishes like paella, tapas, and churros. People often eat churros for breakfast with hot chocolate.

Why People Love Churros

Churros are loved all over the world because they are simple, affordable, and very tasty. They are crispy outside and soft inside.

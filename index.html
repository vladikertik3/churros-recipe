🍩 Churros

Delicious Spanish Dessert

What are churros?

Churros are a sweet fried dessert from Spain. They are crispy outside and soft inside.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup flour

Instructions

  1. Boil water with sugar
  2. Add flour and mix
  3. Let it cool
  4. Fry in oil
  5. Add sugar

History

Churros may have been invented by Spanish shepherds or inspired by Chinese food.

About Spain

Spain is a beautiful country in Europe known for food, culture, and warm weather.
